Strategies for Beginners to Improve Scores

For beginners, a few strategies can greatly improve your scores. Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Timing Your Flaps: Perfecting the timing of your taps is key to avoiding obstacles.

Knowing common mistakes can help you avoid frustration:

  • Ignoring the Environment: Pay attention to how the environment affects your flight.
  • Being Too Aggressive: Rushing can lead to mistakes. Take a more careful approach.
